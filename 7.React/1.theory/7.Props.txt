1.props in react 
*short for properties.
*Mechanism for passing data.
*Read-only by default.

2.Usage.
*Pass data from parent to child componenet.
*Makes componenet reusable.
*Defined as attribute in jsx.


3.Key points
*Data flows one-way (downwords).
*Props are immutable.
*Used for communication between componenets.
